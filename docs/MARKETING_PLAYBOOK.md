## Target Audience

### Primary Segments

**1. Students & Job Seekers (18-30)**
- Need: Professional CVs, study materials, image editing for social media
- Pain Point: Expensive international AI tools, complex interfaces
- Plan: Starter (3 JD) or Pro (5 JD)

**2. Freelancers & Creators (25-40)**
- Need: Client presentations, social media content, video editing
- Pain Point: Multiple subscriptions (Canva, ChatGPT, Midjourney, etc.)
- Plan: Pro (5 JD) or Creator (7 JD)

**3. Small Business Owners (30-50)**
- Need: Marketing materials, proposals, product images
- Pain Point: Can't afford designers, need fast turnaround
- Plan: Creator (7 JD)

**4. Educators & Trainers (25-55)**
- Need: Lesson plans, presentation slides, educational videos
- Pain Point: Time-consuming content creation
- Plan: Pro (5 JD)

### Secondary Segments

- University students preparing for exams
- Social media influencers
- Marketing agencies (small teams)
- NGOs and community organizations
